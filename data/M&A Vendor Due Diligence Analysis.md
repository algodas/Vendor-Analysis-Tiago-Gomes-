## Summary Insights

**Total Annual Spend Analyzed:** $7,919,127

**Action Distribution:**
- **Consolidate:** 77 vendors (vendor overlap, geographic redundancy, functional duplication)
- **Optimize:** 17 vendors (core value but overpriced or underutilized)
- **Terminate:** 6 vendors (low ROI or redundant capabilities)

**Key Findings:**
1. **Office Space Fragmentation:** 12+ office/co-working vendors across regions (Tog, WeWork, Zagrebtower, etc.) - consolidate to 2-3 global providers
2. **Insurance Redundancy:** 8+ insurance vendors with overlapping coverage - consolidate to single global broker
3. **Recruitment Duplication:** 6+ recruitment agencies - consolidate to 2 strategic partners
4. **Consulting Proliferation:** 15+ consulting/advisory vendors - rationalize to core strategic partners
5. **Salesforce Ecosystem:** Largest spend at $3.1M - optimize licensing and usage

**Priority Actions:**
- Consolidate real estate to global provider (save ~$500K-700K annually)
- Rationalize insurance through single broker (save ~$150K-200K)
- Consolidate recruitment to 2 agencies (save ~$100K-150K)
- Optimize Salesforce licensing and adoption (save ~$300K-500K)
